MOBA Games

Here is some information on MOBA games.

What does MOBA stand for?

MOBA stands for Multiplayer online battle arena.

What is a multiplayer online battle arena game?

A type of strategy video game called multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) matches two teams of players against one another on a predetermined battleground. Each player is in charge of a single character with a unique set of skills that develop over a game and aid the team's overall strategy. Each team's ultimate goal is often to demolish the main
structure of their opponent, which is typically located in the other corner of the battlefield.
